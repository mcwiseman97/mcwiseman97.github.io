Design Principles

Proximity - Kayla Roberts —

As you can see, there are three different groupings of words. They each have a larger heading, with a smaller sub-heading.
Also, the spacing between the four images on the top are the same and are very close. While the spacing between those
images and those images at the bottom of the picture is larger, showing that they are separate from each other.

Alignment - Lucas —

Repetition - Michael Wiseman —

Repetition can easily be seen in the main page of Etsy. The most visible form is with color. The website uses orange in many places,
and is used well with different shades of orange so it’s not plain. The use of different shades of orange doesn’t overwhelm the user
but provides a warm comforting feel.
